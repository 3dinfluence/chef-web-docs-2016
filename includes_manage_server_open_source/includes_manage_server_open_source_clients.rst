The ****Management Console**** allows users to create, read, edit, and delete API clients.

Listing Clients
=====================================================
1. Log on to the ****Management Console****.
2. Click **Clients**.

.. image:: ../../images/includes_manage_server_open_source_clients_list.png

Viewing a Specific Client
=====================================================
1. Log on to the ****Management Console**** and click the **Clients** tab.
2. Click on the link of the name of the client you want to view.

.. image:: ../../images/includes_manage_server_open_source_clients_view_1.png

Viewing a Specific Client's Public Key
=====================================================
1. Log on to the ****Management Console**** and click the **Clients** tab.
2. Click on the link of the name of the client you want to view.

.. image:: ../../images/includes_manage_server_open_source_clients_view_1.png

3. Click on the **Show** tab.

.. image:: ../../images/includes_manage_server_open_source_clients_view_2.png

Creating a Client
=====================================================
1. Log on to the ****Management Console**** and click the **Clients** tab.
2. Click **Create** in the sub-navigation menu.

.. image:: ../../images/includes_manage_server_open_source_clients_create_1.png

3. Enter a name, and select whether or not you want the new client to be an admin.

.. image:: ../../images/includes_manage_server_open_source_clients_create_2.png

4. Click **Create Client**.

.. image:: ../../images/includes_manage_server_open_source_clients_create_3.png

5. Save the private key somewhere safe.

Editing a Client
=====================================================
1. Log on to the ****Management Console**** and click the **Clients** tab.
2. Next to the name of the client you want to edit, click the **Edit** link.

.. image:: ../../images/includes_manage_server_open_source_clients_edit_1.png

3. You are allowed to regenerate a private key or change its admin status. Click **Save Client** to save your change.

.. image:: ../../images/includes_manage_server_open_source_clients_edit_2.png

4. If you chose to regenerate the private key, copy and save it somewhere safe.

Deleting a Client
=====================================================
1. Log on to the ****Management Console**** and click the **Clients** tab.
2. Next to the name of the client you want to delete, click the **Delete** link. 

.. image:: ../../images/includes_manage_server_open_source_clients_delete_1.png

Or, click on the name of the client and click **Delete** on the sub-navigation menu. 

3. Click **OK** on the confirmation message box.

.. image:: ../../images/includes_manage_server_open_source_clients_delete_2.png
