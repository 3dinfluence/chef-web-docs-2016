To view details for a |chef validator| key:

#. Open **Chef Manage**.
#. Click |webui policy|.
#. Click |webui policy_client|.
#. Select a |chef validator| key.

   .. image:: ../../images/step_manage_webui_policy_validation_view_details.png

#. Click the |webui tab_details| tab.
