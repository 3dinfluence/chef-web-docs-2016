To filter report histories by |chef client| run status:

#. Open **Chef Manage**.
#. Click |webui reports|.
#. Click |webui reports_run_history|.
#. From the |webui button_filter_by_status| drop-down, select ``All``, ``Success``, ``Failure``, or ``Started``:
