To view permissions for a role object:

#. Open the |chef manage|.
#. Click **Policy**.
#. Click **Roles**.
#. Select a role.
#. Click the **Permissions** tab.
#. Set the appropriate permissions: **Delete**, **Grant**, **Read**, and/or **Update**.
