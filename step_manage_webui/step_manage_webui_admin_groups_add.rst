To add a group:

#. Open the |chef manage|.
#. Click **Administration**.
#. Click **Groups**.
#. Select a group.
#. Click the **Create**.

   .. image:: ../../images/step_manage_webui_admin_groups_add.png

#. Enter the name of the group and then click **Create Group**.
