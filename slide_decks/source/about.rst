 .. revealjs:: Speaker Notes

    You can use speaker notes by adding the ``.. rv_note::`` directive to a slide.

    Hit "s" on your keyboard to view the notes.

    .. code-block:: ruby

       .. rv_note:: 

          This is a speaker note. You found this from your keyboard's "s" key.
