``-c CONFIG_FILE``, ``--config CONFIG_FILE``

   Use to enable stack traces and other debug output. Default value: ``false``.

``-h``, ``--help``
   |help subcommand|

   Use to show policy revisions that are not currently assigned to any policy group.

   |version chef|
