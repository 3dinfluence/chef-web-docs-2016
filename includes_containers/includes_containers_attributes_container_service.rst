Two attributes are used to configure |chef container|:

* ``default['container_service']['apache2']['command'] = "/usr/sbin/apache2 -D FOREGROUND"``
* ``default['container_service']['mysql']['command'] = "/usr/sbin/mysqld -u mysql"``
